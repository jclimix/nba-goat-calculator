Apply Offensive-BOOST?

Meaning: Selecting a boost will apply a multiplier to the OFFENSIVE statistics of all NBA players. This allows fans to apply a sizable BOOST to OFFENSIVE performance.







Apply Defensive-BOOST?

Meaning: Selecting a boost will apply a multiplier to the DEFENSIVE statistics AND accolades of all NBA players. This allows fans to apply a sizable BOOST to DEFENSIVE performance.







Apply Playoffs-BOOST?

Meaning: Selecting a boost will apply a multiplier to the Playoffs statistics of all NBA players. This allows fans to apply a slight BOOST to postseason performance, giving players with higher postseason statistics a higher overall GOAT-Score.

Apply Kudos-BOOST?

Meaning: Selecting a boost will apply a multiplier to the awards and accolades received by all NBA players. This allows fans to place greater emphasis on players that are more accomplished/decorated.







Apply Champion-BOOST?

Meaning: Selecting YES will apply a 4x multiplier to the number of Championships and Finals MVPs for all NBA players. This allows fans to apply a significant BOOST to postseason success, giving players with more Championships and/or Finals MVPs a higher overall GOAT-Score.
